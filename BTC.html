My responsiblities and missions


    I joined Battlecrew: Space Pirates in May 2015 during pre-production as an Intern UI Programmer and left in September 2016 for Twin Mirror. The project shipped in July 2017.

    I prototyped the early UIs like health bars, scores, menus, etc. Then I moved onto helping level designers with gameplay programming tasks, specifically the abilities of the characters.

    As time passed and since my initial passion was design even though I greatly enjoyed programming, I moved onto level design and helped create the maps for the Team Deathmatch mode.

    I briefly helped with design the abilities of the characters that would be released later on before moving on to the next project.
